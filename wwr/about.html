HISTORY

Founded by two rafting enthusiasts, Alex and Jamie, Rapid Rivers Rafting began as a dream on the banks of the Arkansas River. Known for its commitment to safety and fun, the company quickly became a favorite among thrill-seekers. Today, Rapid Rivers Rafting is synonymous with adventure, offering unforgettable experiences on the most challenging and scenic rivers.
